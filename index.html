About Us

Shutterbugs has come a long way since it's inception in 2013 standing as a fulcrum surrounding all the cultural clubs. The events conducted by the Shutterbugs prove to be a scintillating experience for novices , a treat for the interested ones and an art for the experts. Shutterbugs, the photography club of IIT Mandi is one of the most integral and popular clubs at IIT Mandi. The club also conducts various workshops and learning sessions on photography, image-editing and video-editing. As far as photography is concerned, events like Graphic Designing, Photography Competitions, Photo-walks are conducted. There is also a dedicated photoroom or photo studio, in the campus, specially given to Shutterbugs.

History

The very familiar sound of camera clicking was first heard in campus back in 2013 with the commencement of the photography club by Uday Mittal (2014) and Kunal Joshi (2016). It was their persistence and hard work which made photography club a glorious page in the history of IIT Mandi. The flames which were lit by beginners never ceased and photography club is gaining altitude with every passing moment.

Vision

Shutterbugs envisages a vision comprising an outburst of every possible activity, that will help community here to pursue interest in photography. Proposals are abundant, ambitions are high. Pioneering photography tops the list of the club's activities and plans. Your perception towards life here at IIT Mandi will change the day you start handling a camera. The club augment it's technical base as well as the commitment of its members by calling the eminent firms like Nikon to take workshops on the finer aspects of this diverse field of photography. The club intends to grow to a new zenith with a current member base of about 40 people. It even provide the residents of the institute a great opportunity to live up the beautiful hobby. Aquiring sophisticated cameras, lights, powerful specific lenses, filters and highly advanced equipments to arouse interest and enthusiasm in the student community, will be a priority. Finally, "Adding new dimensions to the club" is our motto.
